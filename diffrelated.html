Related Rate

Quick take away

  • Mostly word problem
  • basically Implicit differentations (set up equation)

Pratice

A 13ft ladder leaning agaisnt wall start slide away. By the time base is 12 feet away from wall the base is moving at a rate of 5ft/sec. How fast is the top of ladder sliding down at the moment?

Explain

  1. Determine the equation(this problem is a triangle so used pythagorem theorem)
  2. List the given x=12 dx/dt=5 y=() solved for dy/dt
  3. By pythagorem theorem we got y=5
  4. Implicit differentated the equation
  5. plug in and solved for dy/dt

Note

related rate can also asked in all kind of shape and form(3d) such as sphere cylinder etc.
