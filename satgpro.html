Pronoun Agreement

  1. Singular = it, its, itself
  2. Plural = they, them, their, themselves
  3. The noun to which a pronoun refers (its antecedent or referent) will not always appear in the same sentence

Example

  • Painter Frida Kahlo (1907-1954) was best known for her portraits. They were strongly influenced by indigenous Mexican culture, as revealed by her use of bright colors and dramatic symbolism.
