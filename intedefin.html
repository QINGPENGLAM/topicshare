Definite integral (Antiderivative)

the area under a curve between two fixed limits

Beginning of approximate integral

1. Left riemann sum (b>a , x-value)

(b-a)*f(a)+ ...

2. midpoint riemann sum

(b-a)*f(b-a/2)+...

3. right riemann sum

(b-a)*f(b)+...

Trapizoid riemann sum

(b-a)*(f(b)-f(a))+...

Over and under approximation (ex from above pic)

  • If the bar is under line is underapproximation
  • If the bar is above the line is Overapproximation

note

  • Curve up =f is increasing
  • Curve down= f is decreasing
