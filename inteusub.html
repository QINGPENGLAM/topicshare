U-substitution

Explain

  1. Our goal is to cancel everything out (x)
  2. You can select any u value but at the same time think about du
  3. If we pick x^2 =u then du=dx/2x it would not cancel x^3-7 out
  4. If we choose x^3-7 then du= dx/3x^2 it has x^2 so we used that
  5. Substitute du , cancel x^2
  6. factor out 1/3
  7. integral of u^3 = u^4 /4
  8. subsitute x^3-7 back to u
  9. Answer= ((x^3-7)^4)/12 +C
