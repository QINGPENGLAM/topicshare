Velocity

  • T (particle) Moving to right V(t) postive
  • T move to left V(t) negative
  • T at rest V(t) = 0

Displacement and average velocity

  • Displacement(t from a to b)= x(b)-x(a)
  • AV(t from a to b)= MVT formule

Quick Note

  • Time = f(x)
  • Velocity = f'
  • Acceleration =f''

Speeding up(increasing) and down(decreasing)

  • Speeding up= v(t) and a(t) same sign (both pos or neg)
  • Speeding down = v(t) and a(t) different sign
