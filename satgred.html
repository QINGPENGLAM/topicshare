Wordiness/Redundancy

  1. Shorter is better
  2. the shortest one will virtually always be correct
The construction of the Great Wall of China was an enormous undertaking that occurred on a very large scale and required over 3,000 workers.

Choice

  1. NO CHANGE
  2. an enormously huge undertaking on a large scale
  3. an enormous undertaking, which occurred on a very large scale
  4. an enormous undertaking
